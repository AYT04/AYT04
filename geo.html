Street Detective

The Objective

You are a Street Detective. Your mission is simple but challenging: examine a single photograph from somewhere on Earth and pin its exact location on a world map.

🔍 Observe

Look for license plates, road signs, architecture, or vegetation types.

📍 Pinpoint

Expand the map and place your marker. Accuracy is rewarded with points.

The Creator

Service Providers

Mapillary
